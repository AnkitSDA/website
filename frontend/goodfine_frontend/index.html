About Goodfine

Crafted to Protect. Engineered to Impress.

At Goodfine, we don’t just make wood coatings—we perfect surfaces. Since 2010, we’ve been delivering industry-grade wood finishing solutions that elevate craftsmanship, enhance durability, and bring out the true beauty of wood.

From high-performance wood coatings and rich stains to precision thinners and industrial finishes, our products are trusted by carpenters, furniture manufacturers, interior specialists, and coating professionals across India.

Attractive, volume-based pricing for B2B and bulk buyers, ensuring premium quality at every scale.
