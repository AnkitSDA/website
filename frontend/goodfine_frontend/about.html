About Goodfine

Manufacturer & Supplier of Wood Finishing Solutions

Goodfine is a leading manufacturer and supplier of wood finishing and industrial coating solutions in India, serving distributors, wholesalers, and professional buyers with consistent quality and competitive pricing.

Since 2010, we have been manufacturing PU coatings, NC paints, wood stains, thinners, fillers, and surface preparation products at scale—ensuring better margins, reliable supply, and stable pricing for our distributor partners.
